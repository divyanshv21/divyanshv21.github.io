Course Description

Introduction to topological spaces and their basic properties, topological equivalence and homeomorphisms, continuity and its properties, product and quotient topology, connectedness and compactness, separation axioms and metrization theorems, complete metric spaces and function spaces, introduction to homotopy theory, homotopy of maps and homotopy equivalence.
