Welcome to the MTH403 course page. Here you will find resources related to Complex Analysis.

Course Description

Introduction to complex numbers; Complex functions and their properties; Complex differentiation and Cauchy-Riemann equations; Contour integration and Cauchy's theorem; Taylor and Laurent series; Residue theorem and applications; Conformal mappings; Analytic continuation; Special functions such as Gamma and Zeta functions; Analyticity in harmonic functions.
